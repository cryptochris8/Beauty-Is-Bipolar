Welcome to My Journey

At 27, life threw me a curveball with a bipolar disorder diagnosis. This blog is my canvas to paint the reality of living with a mind that sometimes soars too high and other times plunges too deep.

Through vulnerability and authenticity, I hope to connect with others who understand this journey, educate those who don't, and remind everyone that there is beauty to be found in every perspective – even the bipolar ones.
